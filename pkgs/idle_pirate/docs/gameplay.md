# Idle Pirate - Gameplay Roadmap

This document outlines the exponential gameplay loop and progression system for the Idle Pirate game.

## Phase 1: The Scallywag (Manual Clicking)
At the very beginning, the player has nothing and must do the dirty work themselves. 
* **Primary Currency:** Doubloons.
* **The Action:** Clicking the central target on the screen (for example, swinging a cutlass at a merchant crate or digging a hole).
* **Early Upgrades:** Focus on increasing Doubloons per click. The player buys "Sharper Hooks," "Better Shovels," or "Heavy Boots." 

## Phase 2: The Captain (Early Automation)
Clicking gets tiring. The player's goal shifts from manual labor to building a passive income stream.
* **The Action:** Spending Doubloons to hire a crew.
* **Passive Generators:** 
    * **Cabin Boy:** Generates 1 Doubloon per second.
    * **Gunner:** Generates 15 Doubloons per second.
    * **Quartermaster:** Generates 100 Doubloons per second.
* **The Shift:** The player starts watching the numbers tick up automatically, logging off, and returning to a pile of offline earnings.

## Phase 3: The Armada (Exponential Synergies)
This is where the math goes crazy. Linear growth (just adding more crew) is no longer enough. The player needs multipliers to reach billions and trillions of Doubloons.
* **The Action:** Buying entire ships and global upgrades.
* **Fleets:** Purchasing Sloops, Galleons, and Man-o-Wars that generate massive base income.
* **Synergy Upgrades:** Instead of flat numbers, these are percentage boosts. Buying "Extra Grog Rations" doubles the output of all Cabin Boys. Buying "Reinforced Cannons" makes all Gunners 5x more efficient. 

## Phase 4: Davy Jones' Locker (The Prestige System)
Eventually, the cost of upgrades becomes too high, and progress grinds to a halt. The player must reset the game to advance further.
* **The Trigger:** The player chooses to "Bury the Hoard" or gets "Sunk by the Royal Navy."
* **The Cost:** The player loses all Doubloons, crew, and ships. They start back at Phase 1.
* **The Reward:** They earn a permanent meta-currency based on how much they sacrificed. Let's call it **Infamy** or **Cursed Skulls**.
* **The Hook:** Every point of Infamy gives a permanent 5% global multiplier to all future Doubloon generation. The next playthrough is lightning fast, allowing them to reach entirely new tiers of upgrades.
