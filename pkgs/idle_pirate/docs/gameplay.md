# Idle Pirate - Gameplay and Progression

This document describes the gameplay loop and progression system for the Idle Pirate game.

## Manual Clicking

At the very beginning, the player has nothing and must do the dirty work themselves.
* **Primary Currency**: Doubloons.
* **The Action**: Clicking the central "Open Chest" button on the screen.
* **Equipment**: Focus on increasing Doubloons per click. The player buys "Sharper Hooks," "Better Shovels," or "Heavy Boots."

## Early Automation (The Crew)

Clicking gets tiring. The player's goal shifts from manual labor to building a passive income stream.
* **The Action**: Spending Doubloons to hire a crew.
* **Passive Generators**:
    * **Cabin Boy**: Generates 1 Doubloon every 2 seconds.
    * **Gunner**: Generates 15 Doubloons every 5 seconds.
    * **Quartermaster**: Generates 100 Doubloons every 10 seconds.
* **Visualization**: Each generator type has a filling bar. When the bar fills, the amount is awarded!
* **The Shift**: The player starts watching the numbers tick up automatically, logging off, and returning to a pile of offline earnings.

## Large Scale Automation (The Fleet)

To reach higher tiers of income, the player can buy entire ships.
* **The Action**: Buying entire ships.
* **Fleets**:
    * **Sloop**: Generates 500 Doubloons every 20 seconds.
    * **Brigantine**: Generates 3000 Doubloons every 60 seconds.
    * **Frigate**: Generates 15000 Doubloons every 120 seconds.
